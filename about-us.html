Our Story

Founded in 2003, RF Filters has been at the forefront of electromagnetic interference (EMI) and radio frequency interference (RFI) filter technology. We specialize in designing and manufacturing high-performance filters for critical applications across military, aerospace, medical, and industrial sectors.

Our commitment to excellence and innovation has made us a trusted partner for organizations requiring the highest levels of electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) and interference suppression.
