Specifications

Electrical

  • Current Rating: Withstands 140% of rated current for 15 minutes without deterioration.
  • Insertion Loss: Provides 80 dB minimum insertion loss across indicated frequencies as per MIL-STD-220C.
  • Voltage: Operates continuously at full-rated voltage; withstands 2x rated voltage for 1 minute.

Operating Temperature

  • Filter Element: Assembly: 0°F to 104°F (-18°C to 40°C), Components: 0°F to 150°F (-18°C to 65°C).

Mechanical

  • Case: Made of cold-rolled steel.
  • Finish: Corrosion-resistant lacquer over primer; grounding surfaces plated.
  • Impregnant: Non-flammable as per Underwriters Laboratories.
  • Terminals: High-temp alumina ceramic with flexible insulated lead and flame-retardant lug mount.
