DMC-COIL DISCHARGE UNIT FC-10 000 SERIES

RFI/EMC filters contain large, high quality capacitor sections which can store dangerous charges of electrical energy long after the main source of electrical power has been turned off. All filtered circuits and power supply lines can present a serious hazard to personnel and equipment unless filter capacitors are properly and safely discharged. The capacitor discharge device is a mandatory safety requirement under NEC 460 and NEMA Standard 11-17-1960.
